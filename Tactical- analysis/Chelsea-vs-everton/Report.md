## Shot Outcome Breakdown

Shot outcomes were categorised into:
- Goals
- Shots Saved
- Shots Blocked
- Shots Off Target

Chelsea recorded:
- A higher number of shots resulting in positive outcomes
- Fewer low-value outcomes relative to total shot volume

Everton recorded:
- A higher proportion of non-goal outcomes
- Lower overall conversion relative to shot count

---

## Shot Distance Analysis

Shots were grouped into four distance zones:
- 0–6 yards  
- 6–12 yards  
- 12–18 yards  
- 18+ yards  

### Observations
- Chelsea generated a greater share of shots from shorter distance zones
- Everton’s shot profile skewed more heavily toward longer-range attempts

This distribution aligns with the observed difference in total xG.
